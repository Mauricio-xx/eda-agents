## What the null outcome on FoM means

The 4-bit counter's design space is 4 × 3 = 12 points, budget 2.
Both the proposals-from-skills and proposals-from-bare-prompt paths
converge on the same "best" config the first time they land on a
valid one. FoM being byte-identical across all six runs is a
statement about the **bench's sensitivity**, not about skill
injection: the ceiling is low enough that methodology knowledge has
nothing to bite on. Within this bench, skill content is inert on
outcomes.

Interpreting cautiously:

- **No regression**: the arc is safe to ship. Skill injection does
  not derail the LLM's sampler even when the skill prompts nearly
  double the context the model sees.
- **No positive evidence either**: this bench cannot *confirm*
  skills help. A meaningful A/B needs a harder design where
  valid-design discovery is the bottleneck — e.g. a larger RTL with
  >3-deep design space, or a more stringent validity rule. That's
  follow-on work, not blocking.

## Tokens

Per-run counts are byte-stable across seeds inside a condition, so
the effective sample size on tokens is N=1 per arm. The stable
delta is the skill text itself: `analog.miller_ota_design` +
`analog.gmid_sizing` rendered for the digital counter's
``relevant_skills()`` pulls ~6.4 k extra tokens per proposal call.
Two proposals per run → ~12 k extra tokens per run (matches
observation: 3994 − 1850 = 2144 per call × 2 proposals ≈ 4 300
extra, the remaining ~2 k is first-call system prompt overhead).

## Analog sanity

- **miller_ota**: PASS both sides, delta 0. The deterministic
  callable path remains unaffected by S10c or S10f.
- **sar_adc_11bit**: flipped again (ON FAIL, OFF PASS). The
  comparator's comparator marks the Pass@1 gate FAIL on this task,
  but it is **explicitly flagged as sanity, not gate-relevant** —
  the task does not invoke the LLM at all. Same pre-existing
  SNDR-near-threshold flakiness acknowledged in gap #6b. Not an
  S10c signal. The automated comparator picks it up in the sanity
  block and notes "FAIL" per task; the LLM-backed overall verdict
  is still GREEN.

## Decision

Keep `EDA_AGENTS_INJECT_SKILLS` default at `"1"`. The ±5 % gates
on the LLM-backed task pass with zero slack on FoM and zero slack
on Pass@1. Tokens are expected to rise and the observed 2.16× is
well under the 12 k soft cap.
